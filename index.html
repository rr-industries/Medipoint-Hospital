About Medipoint Hospital

A New Era in Healthcare

A new era started in the Medical world on 14th Dec 2012 with the opening of Medipoint Hospital, the best setup in West Nagpur (New Nagpur) at Manish nagar. It has made history by being the first hospital of its kind to offer comprehensive health care at affordable cost under one roof in this area.

It has 20 beds, spread across 3 floors over an area of more than 3600 sqft. It provides all medical, surgical and super specialty care by renowned doctors using state-of-the art technology.
